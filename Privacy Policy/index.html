ShivLok Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Metagod Creators (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use ShivLok on Meta Quest.

2. No Account or Personal Profile

ShivLok does not require login. We do not collect personal identifiers like name or email.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only aggregate device and usage data via Unity Analytics:

  • Device model & OS version
  • Session duration & interactions
  • Crash reports & performance metrics

4. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to monitor performance, diagnose issues, and plan improvements. We do not use data for advertising or personalization.

5. Data Retention

Unity Analytics retains aggregated logs per its standard policy (up to 180 days). We do not store data beyond that.

6. Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell or rent data. We share non-identifiable aggregates only with Unity Technologies, Meta/Oculus, or legal authorities when required by law.

7. Children’s Privacy

ShivLok is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Your Rights

Under India’s data laws, you can inquire about Unity Analytics data which is non-identifiable and log deletion happens automatically.

9. Future Updates

If we introduce opt-in features (email, push), we will obtain consent and update this policy.
